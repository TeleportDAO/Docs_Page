Introduction

What is TeleportDAO?#

TeleportDAO is a cross-chain settlement protocol with three promising features: trustless, high speed, and simple UX. The main functionalities of TeleportDAO are:

  • Cross-Chain Transfer: users can transfer their assets (tokens, NFTs, and data) from one blockchain (called source chain) to another blockchain (called target chain). This process is two-way, so users can move back their assets from the target chain to the source chain.
  • Cross-Chain Exchange: users can exchange their assets on the source chain for other assets on the target chain. We use automated market maker (AMM) as the price-discovery mechanism.

To deploy TeleportDAO smart contracts, the target chain needs be to programmable, however, the source chain can be programmable or non-programmable. In our protocol, every data is verified by the smart contracts, so, TeleportDAO has blockchain-level security.

TeleportDAO has two special types of settlement:

  • Fast Settlement is a non-collateral-based solution that enables users to perform the cross-chain settlement before their transaction gets finalized on the source chain.
  • Instant Settlement is a collateral-based solution that enables users to perform the cross-chain settlement instantly.

TeleportDAO token is called TDT. TDT is a governance token that is also used to pay rewards to the TeleportDAO's participants.

Why Do We Need TeleportDAO?#

Suppose that Alice has token A on the source chain. Now consider the following scenarios:

  • Transfer Scenarios:

    • Alice wants to pay Bob, a merchant who only accepts token A on the target chain.
    • Alice wants to use a dApp that accepts token A, but it only exists on the target chain.
    • There is a staking pool of token A on the target chain that offers better APY in comparison to staking pools of token A on the source chain.
    • There is no dApp on the source chain (the case of Bitcoin, Dash, etc.), so Alice wants to leverage the target chain dApps.

  • Exchange Scenarios:

    • Alice wants to pay Bob, a merchant who only accepts token B on the target chain.
    • Alice wants to leverage a dApp on the target chain that accepts token B.
    • There is a staking pool of token B on the target chain that offers better APY in comparison to staking pools of token B on the source chain.

In all these cases, Alice needs a cross-chain settlement protocol to transfer her assets to the target chain or exchange them for other assets on the target chain.

How Does TeleportDAO Work?#

In TeleportDAO, users submit their cross-chain transfer and exchange requests on the source chain. Some parties called teleporters collect users' requests from the source chain and submit them on the target chain. The validity of the submitted request is checked by the relay smart contract. Then, valid requests get executed by TeleportDAO smart contracts.

Relay is a secure, decentralized, and universal blockchain interoperability solution. In this solution, parties called relayers submit data from the source chain on the target chain. Relay smart contract checks the correctness of the data. By referring to the submitted data, users can prove that they have locked assets on the source chain and be able to mint an equal amount of assets on the target chain, or prove that they have burnt assets on the target chain to redeem an equal amount of assets on the source chain.

For example, when Alice wants to transfer her Bitcoins from Bitcoin blockchain to Avalanche blockchain, she will send a request on Bitcoin blockchain including the funds she wants to transfer. After a while, she will receive her wrapped Bitcoins on Avalanche blockchain. What happens behind the scenes is that, a teleporter collects Alice's request from Bitcoin and submits it on Avalanche. The relay smart contract on Avalanche will confirm the validity of the submitted request as explained above. Then, TeleportDAO smart contracts will execute the request and send the wrapped assets to Alice's address on Avalanche.

In TeleportDAO, three types of pools exist:

  • Liquidity Pool is a double asset pool that is used for executing exchange requests.
  • Fast Pool is a single asset pool that is used for executing fast cross-chain transfer and exchange requests.
  • Instant Pool is a single asset pool that is used for executing instant cross-chain transfer and exchange requests. To use an instant pool, users need to lock a proper amount of TDT.

A proper incentive mechanism is designed to incentivize all participants including teleporters, relayers, liquidity pool providers, fast pool providers, and instant pool providers to collaborate in TeleportDAO.
